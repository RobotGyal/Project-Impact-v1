Meteor Mass (g) vs. Year

Link

This chart was made to compare the size of a meteor to the year that it fell / was found. The red indicates it was discovered as it was falling. The green indicates that it was found already landed. There does not seem to be much correlation between mass and year, but it does show that several of the older metoer were located while falling, versus, in recent years, more have been found.

This chart compares the number of meteors that are discovered via 'fallen' (found already landed to Earht) or 'fell' (discovered via seeing it falling from the sky). The coloration indicates the mass(g) of the meteor and count is the number of meteors.
